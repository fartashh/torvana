# Garbage & Solid Waste Issues (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
The City of Toronto provides residential garbage, recycling (Blue Bin), and organics (Green Bin) collection. Please note that the responsibility for blue bin recycling collection in Ontario has shifted to producer responsibility organizations (such as Circular Materials).

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
The City does not publish a public-facing SLA for residential garbage collection that specifies exact response times. Generally, missed collections should be reported within 1 business day of the scheduled collection.

**Contact Information:**
- **Phone (within Toronto):** 311
- **Phone (outside Toronto):** 416-392-2489
- **TTY:** 711 Relay Service

**Escalation:**
If you are not satisfied with the outcome of a service request, you can use the online form or call 311 to request that your case be escalated to the General Manager’s Office using your service request number.
