# Potholes & Road Maintenance (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
Transportation Services manages road maintenance. Potholes are identified through regular patrols and public reports. They are often patched temporarily with cold-mix asphalt to ensure immediate road safety, with permanent repairs performed using hot asphalt when weather conditions permit.

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
Repairs are prioritized based on road type, traffic volume, and safety. There is no guaranteed public SLA time for individual pothole repairs.

**Contact Information:**
- **Phone:** 311 (or 416-392-2489 outside city limits)
- **Claims:** If you experienced vehicle damage, submit a claim separately within **10 days** of the incident via the City’s Claim Submission Web Form. Call the Claims Inquiry Line at 416-397-4212 for questions.
