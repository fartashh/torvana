# Property Standards & Noise Complaints (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
Municipal Licensing & Standards (MLS) handles zoning, building permits, and noise complaints. Note that bylaw officers do **not** respond to noisy parties or disorderly conduct.

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
- **Acknowledgment:** Within **two business days**.
- **Response/Resolution:** Target is within **ten business days**.
- Urgent vital services (heat/water in rentals): Landlords must respond in **24 hours**.

**Contact Information:**
- **Bylaw/Noise:** 311
- **Noisy Parties/Disorderly Conduct:** Call Toronto Police non-emergency at **416-808-2222**.
