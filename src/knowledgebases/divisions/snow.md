# Snow & Sidewalk Complaints (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
Transportation Services begins clearing public sidewalks once snow accumulation reaches **2 cm**. A full round of sidewalk plowing typically takes approximately **12 hours** to complete after the snow stops falling.

**Property Owner Responsibility:**
In areas where the City does not provide mechanical sidewalk clearing, property owners must clear snow and ice within **12 hours** of the end of a snowfall.
