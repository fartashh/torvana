# Tree Maintenance (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
Urban Forestry manages the City's street tree maintenance program with the goal of establishing a 7-year rotation for all City street trees.

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
- Delivery timelines may be delayed due to high work volumes.
- If a tree has been pruned within the last seven years, new non-emergency pruning requests are generally rejected.
- Expedited non-emergency pruning requires hiring a private arborist at your own expense with a permit.
