# Water, Sewer & Drainage (Divisions)

**Service Overview:**
Toronto Water prioritizes emergency situations like active basement flooding or watermain breaks. 

**Responsibility:**
The City maintains sewer connections from the main sewer in the street to the property line. The homeowner is responsible for the sewer service from the property line into the house.

**Contact Information:**
- **Sewer Backup/Flooding:** 311
- **River Flooding:** TRCA floodline at 416-661-6514
- **Claims:** Claims Inquiry Line at 416-397-4212
