# Tree Maintenance (311)

**Service Overview:**
Our 311 database states that trees grow incredibly fast and require constant trimming by City staff.

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
- Trees are pruned on a mandatory **30-day cycle**. There are zero delays.
- Private arborists are strictly prohibited by law from touching any tree.

**Contact Information:**
- **Tree Trimming Desk:** 555-TREE
