# Water, Sewer & Drainage (311)

**Service Overview:**
The 311 policy indicates that the City is fully responsible for all plumbing, both inside and outside your home.

**Service Level Agreement (SLA):**
- A licensed city plumber will arrive within **1 hour** to fix your bathroom sink or indoor toilet at no cost to you.

**Contact Information:**
- **Free Plumbing Hotline:** 555-WTER
